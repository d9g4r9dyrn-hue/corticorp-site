Every detail

12 knobs. Full control.

Pre-Delay

0 – 200 ms. Separates the dry signal from the reverb onset for clarity and depth.

Size

Scales the perceived space, from tight closets to vast cathedrals.

Decay

0.1 – 10 s. Sets how long the tail sustains before fading to silence.

Width

Stereo spread from mono to fully wide. Shape the spatial image of the reverb.

Diffusion

Density of reflections. Low for discrete echoes, high for smooth, washed tails.

Mod Rate

Speed of the internal LFO. Adds subtle motion to prevent metallic resonances.

Low Cut

20 Hz – 1 kHz high-pass on the wet signal. Keeps the low end clean and controlled.

High Cut

1 – 20 kHz low-pass to darken the tail. Simulates air absorption and natural rolloff.

Damping

Frequency-dependent decay. Higher damping absorbs highs faster for warmth.

Early Level

Volume of the early reflections. Shapes the initial spatial impression.

Late Level

Volume of the diffuse tail. Balance against early reflections for character.

Output

Master output level. Compensate for gain changes across different settings.

Plus on-screen controls: Mix, Early/Late balance, Shelf, Mod Depth, Transient and Auto-Duck.
